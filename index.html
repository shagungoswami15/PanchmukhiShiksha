FAQs

Q1: What is Panchmukhi Shiksha?
Panchmukhi Shiksha is a unique education system that includes fivefold development.
Q2: How do the Five Fold activities contribute to my overall development?
The Five Fold activities promote physical fitness, practical skills, moral values, intellectual growth, and aesthetic appreciation.
Q3: How can I enroll in activities?
You can register through our website under the enrollment section.
Q4: Is there any fee for participation?
Most activities are free, but some may have minimal charges.
Q5: Which Five Fold activity requires a payment?
Among the Five Fold activities, only the NSS (National Service Scheme) requires a payment.
